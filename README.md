An end-to-end Business Intelligence project built using **Google BigQuery, SQL, Python, DAX, and Power BI** to analyze customer churn, identify revenue at risk, and recommend data-driven retention strategies.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Customer churn directly impacts recurring revenue and long-term business growth. This project demonstrates a complete cloud-based analytics workflow—from data preparation and SQL analysis in Google BigQuery to interactive Power BI dashboards that support executive decision-making.

### Business Objectives

- Analyze customer churn trends
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Quantify revenue at risk
- Build executive dashboards
- Recommend retention strategies

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

### Executive Overview
Provides a high-level view of customer count, revenue, churn rate, and revenue at risk.

### Revenue Intelligence
Analyzes revenue by contract type, internet service, and payment method to identify key revenue drivers.

### Churn Drivers
Explores the factors contributing to customer churn across contracts, services, payment methods, and tenure.

### Retention Opportunities
Highlights high-risk customer segments and quantifies revenue at risk using decomposition analysis.

### Strategic Recommendations
Summarizes business insights and proposes actionable strategies to reduce churn and improve customer retention.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Month-to-Month contracts experienced the highest churn.
- Fiber Optic customers generated the highest revenue but also the highest churn.
- Electronic Check users represented the greatest revenue at risk.
- Customers in their first year were significantly more likely to churn.
- Revenue loss was concentrated within a small number of customer segments.

---

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to migrate to long-term contracts.
- Launch targeted retention campaigns for Fiber Optic customers.
- Promote AutoPay adoption among Electronic Check users.
- Improve first-year customer onboarding.
- Prioritize retention efforts using churn-risk segmentation.
